About

I'm Krissada Wannawanna, a VFX artist based in Thailand specializing in camera matchmoving and rotomation. I've contributed to Hollywood-scale productions at Yannix, one of Asia's leading post-production studios, working on both released titles and upcoming international films.


Grounded in core 3D disciplines: modeling, UV unwrapping, texturing, animation, lighting, and simulation. I bring both creative sensibility and technical precision to every pipeline I work in. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Multimedia Technology and Animation from Mae Fah Luang University.

Well, Whale

Maya Arnold Adobe Substance

Well, Whale was an experimental project for me, focusing on lighting and creating an atmosphere in an underwater landscape, which was a new challenge.

This project was inspired by the movie Black Panther: Wakanda Forever. It’s a piece I’m truly proud of.

Skills Developed:

  • Creating an Underwater Atmosphere: Capturing the essence of the deep ocean.
  • Lighting from Above the Water: Simulating light filtering down from the surface of the water to the ocean floor.
  • Modeling Creatures with Low Poly Count: Designing creatures without excessive faces or polygons.
  • Creating the Underwater Environment: Creating realistic underwater conditions.
